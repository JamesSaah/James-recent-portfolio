With "Our Proud," we aim to inspire a sense of belonging and appreciation for our heritage.

For my recent project, I made a Premium League table using HTML and CSS. It shows stats for ten top teams, like their rank, matches played, wins, draws, and goals against. At the top, you'll see the Premium League logo. It's easy to use and helps fans see how well their favorite teams are doing in the league. With clean formatting and clear organization, this table provides a straightforward way for fans to stay updated on the latest standings and performances. Whether you're a die-hard supporter or just curious about the league, this project offers a user-friendly platform to engage with the exciting world of football.

At the Orange Digital Center, my team and I embarked on an exhilarating project to create a captivating 2D running game using JavaScript and Node.js. Drawing inspiration from classic platformers, we combined our creativity and technical expertise to develop a dynamic gaming experience that would delight players of all ages. Our game featured vibrant graphics, smooth animations, and immersive sound effects, transporting players to a thrilling world filled with obstacles and challenges. Leveraging the power of Node.js, we implemented real-time multiplayer functionality, allowing users to compete against friends or team up for cooperative gameplay. Throughout the development process, we collaborated closely, brainstorming ideas, refining mechanics, and overcoming obstacles together. With each line of code and iteration, we pushed the
boundaries of what was possible, striving to deliver a polished and engaging gaming experience.
